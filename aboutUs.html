Our story starts with you!
Bringing you authentic coffee with flavors from Boston to Brazil and Rwanda

Founded by in 2019 by two friends at Tufts university in their senior year who were coffee fanatics, Kahawa has grown to become a household name in Boston with a plethora of flavors and scents. After Yves and Tinashe graduated from Tufts , Kahwa launched it’s first shop in Mission Hill from their savings and donations from some of their class mates.

We started from Mission Hill and didn’t stop there. Now with six shops, Kahawa is serving multiple communities in the Boston area with a variety of coffee flavors, but the same organic taste that got hundreds of people to fall in love with our coffee in the first place.

We are empowering our communities by making affordable cups of coffee that power your day, employing people who make your coffee with love and care, and using sustainable practices to make sure that most of everything we use goes back into the earth.
