Sustainability

From the way we grow our coffee to how we
serve it - we focus on sustainability

Growing Coffee

Our coffee is collected from Rwanda and Brazil on plantains where
environmentally friendly practices are at the forefront. We irrigate our crops
with springs that flow near the plantains and make sure that pesticides and
other products we use in our crop cycles not harmful to the earth

Serving Coffee

We are proud to say we only use paper cups, plates and bags in all our
locations. By 2023, we are committed to using only paper straws. We limit the
use of plastic cutlery as much as possible by providing steel cutlery at our
locations and using no plastic cutlery during take out.
